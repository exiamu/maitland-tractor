Our Story

Serving Central Florida Since 1972

Don and Nancy Martin opened Maitland Tractor in 1972 and built something rare: a business where the same family has been turning wrenches in the same spot for over fifty years. The lot, the shop, the phone number — all unchanged. The knowledge runs deep.

Today Randy keeps the tradition going. When your tractor goes down, you’re not calling a call center. You’re calling someone who knows exactly what it means when your work stops.
