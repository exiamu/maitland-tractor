Our Services

What We Handle

Repair & Service

Diagnosis and repair for all makes and models of farm tractors and equipment. We work on what’s actually broken — no unnecessary parts, no upselling. Most jobs are completed in the shop, but we can bring the work to you when the machine can’t move.

  • Engine diagnosis and rebuild
  • Transmission and clutch repair
  • Hydraulic systems
  • Electrical troubleshooting
  • PTO and 3-point hitch service
  • Steering and brake work
  • General maintenance and tune-up

Tractor Sales

Quality pre-owned tractors from brands including Massey Ferguson, Kubota, Ford, John Deere, and more. Our inventory rotates — what’s on the lot today may be gone tomorrow. Call us to find out what we currently have, or stop by and walk the yard.

  • Utility tractors — compact to mid-size
  • Loader and backhoe combinations
  • 4WD and 2WD options
  • Multiple makes and models in stock
  • All tractors serviced before sale

Parts

We source parts for all the brands we service. Fifty years in the business means we’ve built the supplier relationships needed to track down components that the big-box stores have never heard of. If we don’t have it on the shelf, we know where to get it.

  • Engine and drivetrain components
  • Hydraulic seals and cylinders
  • Electrical parts and switches
  • Filters, belts, and maintenance items
  • Hard-to-find and discontinued parts sourced

Implements & Attachments

A tractor is only as useful as what you put behind it. We carry box blades, belly mowers, rotary cutters, and other attachments to extend what your equipment can do. We can also advise on what implement fits your tractor and your job.

  • Box blades — multiple sizes in stock
  • Belly mowers and rotary cutters
  • 3-point hitch attachments
  • PTO-driven implements
  • Implement repair and adjustment

We Come to You
